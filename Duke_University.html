Landscaping mimicry

The task was to create a sustainable LEED-certified campus while considering the site issues such as flooding in a natural way by the use of ditches and other ecological landscaping techniques. The client represents Duke University in North Carolina and wished to keep the traditional landscaping settings for this Kunshan Duke University campus. The Phase II campus pedestrian network was made as following the shape of a butterfly. Organic shape for the whole network while each of the campus section containing a more grid-like design.


Dynamics

The client, architecture and landscaping talks were the key parts where I took major lessons from. Taking for example the design of the Student Dormitory Landscaping, the distances between the dorms meeting rooms were designed with a narrow 1.5m distance in between them. Therefore to use maximum space available, the landscape architects team decided to go with a straight rather than organic design.
